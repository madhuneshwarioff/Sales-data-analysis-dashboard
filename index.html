Business Questions

What the project answers

  • What are the overall total sales and total profit?
  • Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
  • Which regions perform best in sales?
  • How do sales trends change over time?
  • Which products are the strongest sellers?

Key Insights

What I discovered

Technology leads revenue

The Technology category generated the strongest overall sales among product categories.

West region stands out

Regional comparison showed the West delivering stronger sales performance than the others.

Discounts can hurt profit

Higher discounts sometimes pushed sales up while reducing profit margins, which is important for pricing strategy.

Dashboard Preview

Interactive result, not just analysis notes

The final dashboard combines KPIs, category comparisons, regional performance, monthly sales movement, product contribution, and customer segment breakdowns into one view.
